What it does

Live intel alerts

Reads your in-game intel channels as reports are posted and turns them into cards — systems, pilots and ships worked out for you — with alerts you tune by distance, gang size and what was said.

Hostile star map

Puts reported hostiles on an interactive map with sovereignty, activity and wormhole overlays, plus travel and capital jump-route planning.

Jabber fleet pings

Read and send fleet pings and chat without leaving the app. The Jabber integration is tuned for the Imperium — its ping format and broadcast channels.

Alpha & feedback

EVE Spai is alpha-state software under active development. Things will break, and that's expected — your feedback and bug reports are very welcome.
